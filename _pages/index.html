Paul Hollywood’s Gingernut Biscuit

22 Oct 2014

I bought the cutest cookie stamp for only $2 at Kmart today. Feeling pretty inspired to bake some biscuits and use my new label, the first dilemma became apparent–what kind of biscuits would allow for the stamp to be seen? Chocolate chip cookies were a no go zone (unfortunately), perhaps short bread? Then I remembered back to high school when I made Gingernut cookies inspired by Athenian coins (I really enjoyed Ancient History…) and had the ancient owl imprinted onto it – they came out perfectly and the owl kept its shape.
